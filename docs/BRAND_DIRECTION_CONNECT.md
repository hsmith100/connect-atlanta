# Connect Brand Direction - Color Scheme

## Brand Strategy Shift

**Key Points:**
- Connect is the primary brand (not Beats on the Beltline)
- Beats on the Beltline is Connect's main product/offering
- Website represents Connect's overall brand identity
- Festival imagery provides vibrant colors naturally
- Site should be professional, clean, with gold accents

---

## Color Palette - Gold & Warm Neutrals

### Primary Color: Connect Gold
**`#D99B2A`** - Rich, warm gold (from Connect logo)
- Use for: CTAs, buttons, important links, key accents
- Provides warmth and premium feel
- High contrast on white backgrounds

### Text & Headers
**`#1A1A1A`** - Near Black / Charcoal
- Use for: Body text, headings
- Excellent readability
- Professional appearance

**`#4A4A4A`** - Medium Gray
- Use for: Secondary text, descriptions
- Good for hierarchy

### Background Colors

**`#FFFFFF`** - Pure White
- Primary background
- Clean, open feeling

**`#F9F7F4`** - Warm Off-White / Cream
- Subtle sections
- Warmer than pure white
- Complements gold beautifully

**`#F2EDE6`** - Light Warm Gray / Sand
- Alternate sections
- Creates depth without being dark
- Warm neutral that pairs with gold

**`#E8E3DB`** - Warm Gray / Taupe
- Borders, dividers
- Cards with subtle background
- Footer background option

### Accent Colors

**`#B8860B`** - Dark Gold
- Hover states for gold buttons
- Secondary gold elements
- Deeper, richer variation

**`#2C2C2C`** - Dark Charcoal
- Hero sections with light text
- Footer background
- High contrast areas

**Optional Accent (for links/hover):**
**`#18B4DD`** - Cyan (keep as secondary accent)
- Provides cool contrast to warm palette
- Links, icons
- Not primary, but useful for variety

## Color Usage Guide

### Buttons & CTAs
```
Primary Button:
- Background: #D99B2A (gold)
- Text: #1A1A1A (dark) or #FFFFFF (white) depending on contrast
- Hover: #B8860B (dark gold)
- Border: Optional 2px solid

Secondary Button:
- Background: transparent
- Text: #D99B2A (gold)
- Border: 2px solid #D99B2A
- Hover: Background #D99B2A, Text white
```

### Sections
```
Hero Sections:
- Background: #2C2C2C (dark charcoal) with light text
- OR Background: #FFFFFF with image overlay
- CTA: Gold buttons

Content Sections (Alternating):
1. #FFFFFF (pure white)
2. #F9F7F4 (warm cream)
3. #F2EDE6 (light sand)
4. #FFFFFF (pure white)
```

### Cards
```
White cards with warm shadows:
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Border: 2px solid #E8E3DB or #D99B2A (gold for hover)
- Shadow: subtle warm shadow
- Hover: Border becomes gold
```

### Typography
```
Headings:
- Color: #1A1A1A (charcoal)
- Weight: 700-900 (bold to black)
- Font: font-title (Montserrat)

Body Text:
- Color: #4A4A4A (medium gray)
- Weight: 400-500
- Line height: 1.6-1.7

Links:
- Default: #D99B2A (gold)
- Hover: #B8860B (dark gold)
- Visited: Same as default
```

## Design Principles

### 1. **Let Festival Images Shine**
- Minimal background colors
- White space around images
- Let event photos provide the vibrant colors
- Gallery images are the "rainbow" element

### 2. **Gold as Accent, Not Dominant**
- Use sparingly for CTAs and important elements
- Don't overuse - keeps it premium
- Reserve for actions and key highlights

### 3. **Warm Neutrals Create Sophistication**
- Cream and warm grays feel premium
- More sophisticated than pure gray
- Complements gold without competing

### 4. **High Contrast for Readability**
- Dark text on light backgrounds
- Never gold text on white (poor contrast)
- Always test accessibility

### 5. **Professional & Clean**
- Lots of white space
- Organized layouts
- Clear hierarchy
- No busy patterns

## Contrast Ratios (Accessibility)

### AAA Compliant Combinations:
✅ #1A1A1A text on #FFFFFF (21:1)
✅ #1A1A1A text on #F9F7F4 (19:1)
✅ #4A4A4A text on #FFFFFF (9:1)
✅ #FFFFFF text on #2C2C2C (16:1)

### AA Compliant:
✅ #D99B2A on #FFFFFF (5.8:1) - Large text OK
⚠️ #D99B2A on #FFFFFF - Use dark text in gold buttons for better contrast

## Implementation Priority

### Phase 1: Core Colors
1. Update primary color to gold
2. Update backgrounds to warm neutrals
3. Update text colors to dark
4. Update button styles

### Phase 2: Refinement
1. Add warm shadows
2. Adjust card borders
3. Update form inputs
4. Refine hover states

### Phase 3: Polish
1. Test all contrast ratios
2. Add subtle gradients (warm)
3. Optimize for brand consistency
4. Final accessibility check
